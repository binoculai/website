Welcome to Binoculai

Here's what you need to know about our Mail Search AI Google Workspace Addon and Google Chat app:

What we do with your data

Our apps access your Gmail data, process your email content and metadata, and create semantic embeddings. These embeddings are used to provide semantic search functionality across your Gmail. We save Gmail message IDs to fetch the content of your top search results when you conduct a search. We also collect usage information to understand usage patterns, manage subscriptions, and improve our services. Your data is never shared with any third-party services except for the OpenAI API.

Enhancing user functionality

Our apps significantly improve your Gmail experience by providing a robust semantic search functionality. You can conduct searches through our Gmail Addon or Google Chat app, and our system will fetch the most relevant results from your emails. We utilize the OpenAI API to process these results and deliver them to you in a natural language response.

By using our apps, you are giving your explicit consent for us to use and share your data as described. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at support@binoculai.top.
